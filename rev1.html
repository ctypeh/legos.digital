Early Days

Originally called Cox Blocks Rocks (coxblocks.rocks), this server was created in March of 2020 as the official minecraft server for the Do By Friday Podcast, to give people a pleasant distraction from COVID-19 with the comforting nostalgia of Minecraft. It was originally being run by Matt, before being handed to Claire and Link as a community project in June. Everything—including spawn and all 15k+ blocks distance (the actual total volume excavated is probably closer to 500k) of underground railway tunnel—was hand-build in survival.
