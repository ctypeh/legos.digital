Early Days

This server was created in March of 2020 as the official minecraft server for the Do By Friday Podcast, to give people a pleasant distraction from COVID-19 with the comforting nostalgia of Minecraft. It was originally being run by Matt, before being handed to Claire and Link as a community project in June.
